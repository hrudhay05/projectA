Gokaraju Rangaraju Institute of Engineering and Technology

Gokaraju Rangaraju Institute of Engineering and Technology (GRIET) is established in 1997 by Dr. G Gangaraju as a self-financed institute under the aegis of Gokaraju Rangaraju Educational Society. GRIET is approved by AICTE, New Delhi, permanently affiliated to and autonomous under JNTUH, Hyderabad. GRIET is committed to quality education and is known for its innovative teaching practices.

Courses We Offer

Gokaraju Rangaraju Institute of Engineering and Technology (GRIET) offers UG and PG courses in numerous specializations. It offers B. Tech and M. Tech programme. Candidate can apply to this Institute online. Admission in the courses are based on Entrance Examination

B.Tech

The eligibility criteria for B. Tech programme is 10+2 passed with Science stream,For B. Tech, the scores in the EAMCET entrance exam is required.

M.Tech

For M.Tech, the scores of the GATE entrance exam is required. Otherwise, the scores in the PGECET exam would also be accepted during admissions.

B.Tech

The eligibility criteria for B. Tech programme is 10+2 passed with Science stream,For B. Tech, the scores in the EAMCET entrance exam is required.
